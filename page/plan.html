产品与服务 Products & Services

快速实验平台 Rapid Experiment Platform

为“微决策”场景设计的敏捷测试工具。例如，测试两个短视频封面哪个更吸引人，平台可在5-15分钟内完成小样本AB测试，提供清晰可靠的数据反馈，无需用户掌握专业知识。 An agile testing tool designed for "micro-decision" scenarios. For example, to test which of two short-video covers is more appealing, the platform can complete a small-sample A/B test in 5-15 minutes, providing clear and reliable data feedback without requiring professional knowledge.

AI 访谈系统 AI Interview System

用 AI 主持第一轮大规模用户访谈，7x24小时不间断工作。系统能进行标准化提问，并对回答进行实时转录与初步的情感分析、关键词提取。 Uses AI to conduct the first round of large-scale user interviews, operating 24/7. The system can ask standardized questions and perform real-time transcription, preliminary sentiment analysis, and keyword extraction on the responses.

“虚拟用户”模拟系统 "Virtual User" Simulation System

基于真实、脱敏用户画像数据训练的 AI 模型，能模拟真实用户行为与反馈，为产品优化、内容测试等提供高效、可扩展、可反复测试的“模拟沙箱”。 An AI model trained on real, anonymized user profile data. It can simulate real user behavior and feedback, providing an efficient, scalable, and repeatable "simulation sandbox" for product optimization and content testing.

一站式智能研究平台 All-in-One Intelligent Research Platform

我们的终极形态。整合所有功能，并延伸至研究计划撰写、问卷生成、数据处理，直至一键生成可视化报告，全面实现研究流程的智能化与自动化。 Our ultimate form. It will integrate all functions and extend to more stages of the research chain, including AI-assisted research plan writing, automatic questionnaire generation, data processing, and one-click generation of visual reports, fully automating the research process.

我们的优势与解决方案 Our Advantages & Solutions

透明化品控：Transparent Quality Control: 明确告知样本画像、来源，并提供“样本质量分”。Clearly state sample profiles, sources, and provide a "sample quality score."

科学化呈现：Scientific Presentation: 提供置信区间、统计显著性等关键指标，并用通俗语言解释。Provide key metrics like confidence intervals and statistical significance, explained in plain language.

清晰的预期管理：Clear Expectation Management: 报告自动附上提醒，强调结果仅供方向性参考。Reports automatically include a reminder that results are for directional reference only.

“人机混合”访谈模式："Human-Machine Hybrid" Interview Mode: AI 担任“超级助教”，执行第一轮标准化访谈。AI acts as a "super assistant" for the first round of standardized interviews.

智能筛选与标记：Intelligent Screening & Tagging: AI 自动标记出情绪波动明显、内容丰富的“高价值”用户。AI automatically tags "high-value" users with significant emotional fluctuations or rich content.

专家聚焦深度：Expert-Focused Depth: 真人研究员直接对被筛选出的关键用户进行深入追访。Human researchers conduct in-depth follow-ups with key users screened by AI.

持续验证与校准：Continuous Validation & Calibration: 建立严格的“人机对比”验证机制，定期用真实用户“考核”虚拟用户，并公开“可信度报告”。Establish a strict "human-machine comparison" validation mechanism, regularly "evaluating" virtual users with real users and publishing "credibility reports."

引入“边缘”变量：Introducing "Edge" Variables: 刻意创建代表“非主流”行为的虚拟用户，以对抗主流偏见。Intentionally create virtual users representing "non-mainstream" behaviors to counter mainstream bias.

强调辅助定位：Emphasizing an Auxiliary Role: 始终强调“虚拟用户”是高效的假设验证工具，而非真实用户的完美替代品。Always emphasize that "virtual users" are efficient hypothesis-testing tools, not perfect substitutes for real users.

引导式研究设计：Guided Research Design: AI 像教练一样，通过提问引导用户厘清业务问题、研究目标和决策用途。AI acts like a coach, guiding users to clarify business problems, research objectives, and decision-making purposes through questions.

报告的教育性解读：Educational Report Interpretation: AI 报告不仅有结论，更有解读和提醒，主动提示潜在的解读误区。AI reports provide not just conclusions but also interpretations and reminders, proactively highlighting potential interpretation pitfalls.

商业模式 Business Model

按次付费 Pay-as-you-go

针对个人用户和临时需求，提供“快速实验”等单次服务，价格亲民，即用即付。 For individual users and temporary needs, offering single services like "Rapid Experiments" at affordable, pay-per-use prices.

订阅制 Subscription

面向中小企业和高频使用者，推出不同等级的订阅套餐，解锁更高级的功能。 For SMEs and frequent users, offering tiered subscription plans that unlock more advanced features.

企业解决方案 Enterprise Solution

为大型企业提供定制化的私有部署、技术整合及专属研究咨询支持。 Providing large enterprises with customized private deployments, technical integration, and dedicated research consulting support.
